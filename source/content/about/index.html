About the author and contributors

The author

IDNA convert is written and maintained by Matthias Sommerfeld. He is a (freelance) web developer since 2000. His professional profile can be found over at LinkedIn, his company's website (in German) is here.

The first version of IDNA Convert came to light in 2004, when IDN domains became available in Germany. The goal was to easily handle IDN domains in various projects. Since PHP does not support IDN domains in the core, it was necessary to code the algorithm in pure PHP.

To get in touch with Matthias, email him: matthias [dot] sommerfeld [at] algo26 [dot] de.
